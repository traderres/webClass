Where to find Hardened Containers
---------------------------------


Procedure
---------
 1. Register for an account with Iron Bank
    https://ironbank.dso.mil/about
    NOTE:  You will need to provide an email address

 2. Login with your new account

 3. Search their hardened image catalog
    https://ironbank.dso.mil/repomap

 4. Search their hardened image catalog for openjdk
    https://ironbank.dso.mil/repomap?searchText=openjdk&page=1&sort=1&cardsPerPage=10


Hardened Images
---------------
 A) openjdk17
    https://ironbank.dso.mil/repomap/details;registry1Path=redhat%252Fopenjdk%252Fopenjdk17?page=1&sort=1&cardsPerPage=3
    docker pull registry1.dso.mil/ironbank/redhat/openjdk/openjdk17:1.17

 B) openjdk17-slim / OpenJDK 17 JRE on a Red Hat UBI 8 Minimal Base Image
    https://ironbank.dso.mil/repomap/details;registry1Path=redhat%252Fopenjdk%252Fopenjdk17-slim?page=1&sort=1&cardsPerPage=3
    docker pull registry1.dso.mil/ironbank/redhat/openjdk/openjdk17-slim:1.17
