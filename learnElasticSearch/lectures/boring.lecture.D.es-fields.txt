Boring Lecture:  ElasticSearch Fields
-------------------------------------


ElasticSearch Fields
--------------------
 1. Keyword     For searching whole words / status labels
                + you can apply aggregates against it

 2. Text:       For searching paragraphs of text (full-text search)
                + case insensitive searches
                + supports wildcard searches
                - you cannot do aggregates on it

 3. boolean     For holding true/false values
