Problem:  Adobe Acrobat Reader is Really Slow
Solution: Turn off "Use Page Cache"


Procedure
---------
 1. Startup Acrobat Reader App
 2. In the upper left corner:  Menu -> Preferences   (or press Ctrl-K)
 3. On the left, choose "Page Display"
 4. In the Rendering Section, Uncheck "Use page cache"
 5. Press "OK"
 6. Reboot Windows / THIS IS IMPORTANT!!!
