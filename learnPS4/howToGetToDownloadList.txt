How to View Download List
-------------------------

Problem:  You want to re-download a game to your PS4 (or you entered the redeem code but the software will not download)

Solution: 
  1) Go to the "Download List" page
     https://store.playstation.com/#!/en-us/download/list?smcid=ps:shop-now:ps-psn:playstation-store
     
  2) Scroll Down to your game
  
  3) Press "Download"
