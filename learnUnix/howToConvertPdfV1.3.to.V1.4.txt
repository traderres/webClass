How to Convert a PDF V1.3 to a PDF V1.4
---------------------------------------
Problem:  My Canon MX922 scanner scans PDFs to the V1.3 format
          But, I need it to generate a V1.4 PDF


Solution: Use Ghostscript to convert it
---------------------------------------
unix> gs -sDEVICE=pdfwrite -dCompatibilityLevel=1.4 -dNOPAUSE -dQUIET -dBATCH -sOutputFile=OUTPUT.v1.4.pdf INPUT.v1.3.pdf     # Generates OUTPUT.v1.4.pdf
