How to Show Costco Reviews
--------------------------

Problem:  The costco reviews are invisible in my Firefox browser
Solution: Turn on "Personalized Advertising"


Procedure
---------
 1. Open a browser
 2. Go to costco.com
 3. Scroll down to footer on costco site where you can see "Cookie Settings"
 4. Click on it and then click on "Personalized Advertising"
 5. On the upper right corner click to activate
 6. Click on "Confirm My Choices"
