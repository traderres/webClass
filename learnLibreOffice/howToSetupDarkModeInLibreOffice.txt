How to Turn on "Dark Mode" in LibreOffice
-----------------------------------------
Problem:  I upgraded LibreOffice and lost my dark mode
          NOTE:  These procedures were tested in LibreOffice 25.2.5.2

Procedure
---------
 1. Startup any of the LibreOffice applications

 2. Pull Tools -> Options

 3. In the massive Options tab
    a. On the left, Choose LibreOffice -> Appearance
    b. In the upper right corner, you will see 3 radio buttons
       -- Select the "Dark" option for Appearance
    c. Press Apply
    d. Press OK
