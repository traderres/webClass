How to Customize 2022 BMW 740i
------------------------------
Problem:  I want to reduce the fake engine noise
          I want the auto-start/auto-stop engine to remember the last settings (instead of turning on auto-start every time)

Procedure
---------
 1. Purchase the vLinker BM+
    -- It costs about $30

 2. Download the "BimmerCode" App
    -- The full version costs $50

 3. Turn off the engine

 4. Connect the VLinker BM+ device to the OBD port (near the left foot rest)

 5. Turn on the blue tooth on your android phone
    -- Connect to vLinker-BM-Android
    -- The pair code is 1234

 6. Start the "BimmerCode" app
    a. Press "Connect"
    b. Select vLinker Bluetooth and press "Connect"
       -- The app will start talking to your BMW
       -- It will put the car into diagnostic mode

    c. Change the Engine Start/Stop to Remember last setting

    d. Press "Code"
       -- This will save the settings
